### What I do

I build business operations software — dashboards, booking systems, internal
tools — and wire AI directly into the workflow instead of adding it as a
chatbot bolted on the side.

If you're hiring for someone who understands both the backend of a business
and how to put AI to work inside it, that's the work I do.

<br clear="right"/>

---

### 🧭 About

Most "full-stack" profiles list five frameworks and no direction. Mine
doesn't. I work at the intersection of two things:

- **Business operations software** — inventory, ticketing, booking, admin
  dashboards, the tools companies run their day on
- **Applied AI integration** — using LLM APIs to remove manual steps from
  those workflows: auto-categorization, smart replies, document parsing,
  decision support

Two years of focused, hands-on experience taught me one thing clearly:
businesses that treat AI as core infrastructure today are the ones setting
the pace tomorrow. I build at exactly that intersection.
